--- title: "Carbon Pricing & Trade" excerpt: "Empirical analysis of how carbon pricing and CBAM affect trade in energy-intensive, trade-exposed sectors." collection: portfolio --- ### Overview Analysed sector-level trade and carbon pricing data to explore how carbon taxes and the EU ETS/CBAM affect exports in energy-intensive, trade-exposed sectors. ### What I did - Merged trade data with carbon pricing indicators across multiple jurisdictions. - Built sector-level panels and visualised trends in carbon prices vs exports. - Explored differences between covered vs non-covered sectors. - Summarised findings for coursework / dissertation work. ### Methods & tools - **Econometrics / stats:** panel data analysis, visualisation - **Software:** R / Python (pandas, ggplot / matplotlib) - **Data:** trade flows, World Bank Carbon Pricing Dashboard, EU ETS / CBAM info
