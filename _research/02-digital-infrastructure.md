## Abstract

This report maps the digital infrastructure underpinning EU ETS and CBAM compliance systems — from emissions monitoring and verification platforms to border carbon adjustment reporting. It proposes a cyber capacity-building framework to help African trade partners meet CBAM digital reporting requirements, combining gravity model analysis of EU–Africa trade flows with an assessment of digital governance readiness.
